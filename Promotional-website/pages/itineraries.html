Sample Itineraries

1-Day Adventure
  • Morning: Visit Pagsanjan Falls for a boat ride and swim
  • Lunch: Try local cuisine in Pagsanjan town
  • Afternoon: Explore Paete for woodcarving and art
  • Evening: Relax in a hot spring resort in Calamba or Los Baños
2-Day Nature & Culture
  • Day 1: Hike Mount Makiling, visit the UPLB campus, and enjoy Los Baños hot springs
  • Day 2: Tour the Seven Lakes of San Pablo, visit Rizal Shrine in Calamba, and shop for buko pie
