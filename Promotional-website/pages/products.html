Explore Laguna's unique products and delicious delicacies

Famous Laguna Products

Buko Pie

A must-try coconut pie, famous in Los Baños and sold in many shops along the highway.

Espasol

A sweet, chewy rice cake dusted with toasted rice flour, perfect as a pasalubong (souvenir).

Woodcrafts & Embroidery

Handmade woodcarvings from Paete and embroidered garments from Lumban are prized local crafts.

Where to Buy

  • Public markets and souvenir shops in major towns
  • Roadside stalls along the national highway
  • Specialty stores in Los Baños, Lumban, and Paete
  • Festival booths during local celebrations
