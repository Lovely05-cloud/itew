Laguna's Colorful Festivals

Experience the vibrant celebrations and traditions of Laguna

Major Festivals in Laguna

Anilag Festival

Held every March in Sta. Cruz, this week-long festival showcases the best of Laguna's culture, products, and people through parades, street dancing, and exhibits.

Pagsanjan Bangkero Festival

Celebrated in March, this festival honors the boatmen (bangkeros) of Pagsanjan with river parades, boat races, and cultural shows.

Cavinti Waterfalls Festival

A celebration of Cavinti's natural wonders, featuring eco-tours, adventure races, and local delicacies.

Sampaguita Festival

Held in San Pedro every February, this festival pays tribute to the national flower with floral floats, street dancing, and art exhibits.

Why Join a Festival?

  • Immerse in local culture and traditions
  • Enjoy colorful parades and street dancing
  • Taste authentic Laguna delicacies
  • Meet friendly locals and make new friends
  • Capture unforgettable memories
