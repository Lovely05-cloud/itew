Famous Arts & Crafts

Paete Woodcarving

The town of Paete is renowned for its intricate woodcarvings, sculptures, and religious icons, crafted by skilled local artisans.

Lumban Embroidery

Lumban is famous for its fine hand-embroidered barong Tagalog and other traditional Filipino garments.

Paper Mache (Taka)

The colorful paper mache horses and figures from Paete are popular souvenirs and festival icons.

Meet Local Artists

Laguna is home to many talented painters, sculptors, and craftsmen. Visit local workshops and galleries to see their work and support their craft!
