Adventure Activities

Thrilling adventures and outdoor fun await in Laguna

Top Adventure Activities

Waterfall Trekking

Explore hidden waterfalls like Pagsanjan Falls and Hulugan Falls for a refreshing adventure.

Hiking & Nature Trails

Hike up Mount Makiling or Mount Banahaw for breathtaking views and encounters with local flora and fauna.

Lake Activities

Try kayaking, paddle boarding, or fishing in the Seven Lakes of San Pablo or Lake Caliraya.

Safety Tips

  • Always go with a guide for remote or challenging trails
  • Bring enough water, snacks, and sun protection
  • Wear proper footwear and gear
  • Respect nature and local communities
  • Check weather conditions before your trip
