History & Heritage

Discover the stories and legacy of Laguna

A Glimpse into Laguna's Past

  • Pre-colonial Era: Early settlements around Laguna de Bay, thriving trade and culture.
  • Spanish Period: Establishment of towns, churches, and the spread of Christianity.
  • 19th Century: Birthplace of Dr. Jose Rizal, the Philippine national hero.
  • Modern Day: Laguna as a center for industry, tourism, and culture in Southern Luzon.

Notable Heritage Sites

Rizal Shrine

The restored ancestral home of Dr. Jose Rizal in Calamba, now a museum dedicated to his life and works.

Nagcarlan Underground Cemetery

A unique Spanish-era cemetery with an underground crypt, declared a National Historical Landmark.

Pagsanjan Church

A centuries-old church known for its beautiful architecture and historical significance.

Did you know? Laguna is home to more than 30 Spanish-era churches and heritage houses, making it a treasure trove for history buffs!
