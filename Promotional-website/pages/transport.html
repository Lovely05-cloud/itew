Transportation & Getting There

How to reach and travel around Laguna with ease

How to Get to Laguna

  • By Bus: Regular buses from Metro Manila (Buendia, Cubao, Alabang) to major Laguna towns.
  • By Car: Take SLEX (South Luzon Expressway) and exit at Calamba, Sto. Tomas, or other Laguna exits.
  • By Van: UV Express vans from various terminals in Metro Manila.
  • By Train: PNR (Philippine National Railways) has limited service to some Laguna towns.

Getting Around Laguna

  • Jeepneys and tricycles are the main modes of local transport
  • Buses and vans connect major towns and cities
  • Car rentals and ride-hailing apps are available in urban areas
  • Biking is popular in scenic and rural areas
