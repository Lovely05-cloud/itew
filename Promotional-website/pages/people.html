Famous People

Meet the notable personalities who hail from Laguna

Notable Lagunenses

Dr. Jose Rizal

The Philippine national hero, born in Calamba, Laguna. His writings and life inspired the country's fight for independence.

Paciano Rizal

A revolutionary general and the elder brother of Jose Rizal, also from Calamba.

Teodoro Kalaw

A respected writer, historian, and statesman from Lipa, Laguna.

Fernando Poe Jr.

National Artist for Film, with roots in San Pablo, Laguna.
